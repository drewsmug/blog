Ham and Cheese Melt

Ingredients

Sourdough Break 2 slices
Swiss Cheese 4 slices
Black Forest Ham 4 slices
Basil Leaves
Roma Tomato
Butter

Instructions

  • Slice the roma tomato
  • Two slices of swiss cheese should go against each side of bread
  • Add the black forest ham, basil leaves, and roma tomato between swiss cheese
  • Butter one side of the sandwhich
  • Prehead an indoor pan over low heat
  • Cover with lid or foil to keep the head in
  • Cook for about 8 minutes or until the bottom layer of cheese melts and the grilled side is golden brown
  • Right before you turn the sandwiches over, spread more butter, generously on the second side
  • Cover and cook the sandwiches for an additional 6 minutes or until the cheese in completely melted and the bread is a beautiful golden brown

Notes

  • The break, ham, cheese, and tomato can be substituted for whatever your preference is
