Orange Julius

Ingredients

Orange Juice Concentrate 12 oz can
Whole Milk 1 1/2 cup
Sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Dried Egg White 1 tsp
Ice 1 1/2 cup

Instructions

  • Mix all the ingredients (except for the ice) in a blender until smooth
  • Add the ice and mix to desired texture

Notes

  • Garnish with an orange wheel and whip cream
  • We could try half & half in place of milk
