Sushi Rice

Ingredients

Rice Vinegar 800 ml
Sugar 300 g
Salt 150 g
Kombu 7 g

Instructions

  • Heat and mix all together. Do not bring to a boil

Notes

  • Rice to vinegar ratio: 3 parts uncooked rice 1 part sushi vinegar
  • Probaby last about a month in the pantry
  • You can use white or brown sugar
